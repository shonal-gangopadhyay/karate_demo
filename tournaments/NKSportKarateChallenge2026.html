NK Sport Karate Challenge

Registration - ask your school Director for assistance if needed

Students who are in the little masters program (4-6 year olds) do not usually have a form that they can compete with. Instead, they may compete with the regular moves that they practice in class! Students whose rank range from white to red with brown stripe may also compete in the basics division. Judges consider:

  • Clean, deep stances and correct technique
  • Consistent speed
  • Power
  • Loud, controlled yells
  • Focused eyes and strong intent
